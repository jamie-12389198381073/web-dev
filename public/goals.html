Sustainability Goals

Industry

Our goal is to facilitate a sustainable future through enacting three UN sustainability goals: Industry, Innovation and No Poverty. Industry is essential to the functioning of the world and it requires talent to generate technology that can then be used to support humanity.

Innovation and Infrastructure

Innovation and Infrastructure are two crucial technologies that are required to ensure that our technologies can support us through systems such as transport and electricity. Innovation requires a good educational system as well as a dedicated workforce committed to solving the most serious problems in todays world.

No Poverty

Poverty is a serious problem in both the developed and developing world. It is essential that systems are in place to prevent poverty that can lead to secondary consequences such as malnutrition and any form of involuntary harm.
